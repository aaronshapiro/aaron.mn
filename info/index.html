Aaron Shapiro is a product designer in Brooklyn, New York.

E: aaron@aaron.mn

Nice to meet you.

Most recently, I helped make Internet access affordable and accessible at Karma – a company offering a simple wireless hotspot controlled through its mobile apps and website.

Before Karma, I redesigned Cover – an iOS app for making dining out easier in New York City, San Francisco, and Los Angeles. Our launch coincided with months of user testing sessions, prototyping interactions with Flinto and Origami, and designing and building a new marketing website.

I spent two years as a staff product designer at Etsy. I helped redesign its web-based seller tools and led an effort to replace aging CSS with a living, component-based style guide.

I've also worked for Huge's product design team on projects for HBO, American Express, OkCupid, Newsweek, and Twitter.

Aside from work, I love to take photos around New York City and abroad, shake a healthy number of classic daiquiris, and continue to wait patiently for a successful Minnesota Timberwolves season. Good inspiration comes from experience. I try to stand often and spend time away from my desk. It helps.
