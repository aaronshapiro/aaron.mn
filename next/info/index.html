Hi. I'm Aaron, a designer living in Brooklyn, New York.

E: aaron@aaron.mn

I most recently worked on Cover, an iOS app for making dining out easier. You can read about my experience here.

Before Cover, I spent two years as a staff product designer at Etsy. I helped redesign its web-based seller tools and led an effort to replace aging CSS with a living style guide.

I've also worked for Huge's product design team on projects for HBO, American Express, OkCupid, Newsweek, and Twitter.

Aside from work, I love to take photos around New York City and abroad. Good inspiration comes from experience. I try to stand often and spend time away from my desk. It helps.
