Aaron Shapiro is a designer in Brooklyn, New York.

E: aaron@aaron.mn

Nice to meet you.

I most recently worked on Cover, an iOS app for making dining out easier. I wrote about my experience here.

Before Cover, I spent two years as a staff product designer at Etsy. I helped redesign its web-based seller tools and led an effort to replace aging CSS with a living style guide.

I've also worked for Huge's product design team on projects for HBO, American Express, OkCupid, Newsweek, and Twitter.

Aside from work, I love to take photos around New York City and abroad, make cocktails that lean dark and stirred, and continue to wait patiently for a successful Minnesota Vikings season. Good inspiration comes from experience. I try to stand often and spend time away from my desk. It helps.
